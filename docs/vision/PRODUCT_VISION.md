# Thomas OS — Product Vision

**Thomas** is a modern operating system for premium retail brands.

**Chosen by Chloe** is the first business running on Thomas.

Thomas is the operational backbone — a single platform that manages products, inventory, orders, fulfilment, procurement, and future sales channels.

## Principles

1. **Single source of truth** — Inventory lives in Thomas, not spreadsheets.
2. **Immutable audit trail** — Every stock change is logged; balances are derived from movements.
3. **Channel-agnostic** — Shopify, WeChat, Amazon, and Xiaohongshu connect to the same product master.
4. **Warehouse-first** — Stock is tracked per location, supporting multiple warehouses.
5. **Platform vs tenant** — Thomas owns domain logic; each retailer owns branding and channel config.
